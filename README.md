## About

Vanilla bone meal is useful, but painfully slow for large-scale terraforming. Super Bone Meal fixes that by applying a massive bone meal effect to a 50x50 area around the player in a single right-click.

It also includes a precision growth mechanic for arborists — saplings, bamboo, and sugar cane are brute-forced to their final growth stage instantly.

---

## Features

- Applies bone meal effect to a 50x50 area around the player on right-click
- Instantly grows saplings, bamboo, and sugar cane to their final stage
- Simple crafting recipe — no complex setup required

---

## Crafting

Craft with **9 bone blocks** in a full crafting grid.

```
[Bone Block] [Bone Block] [Bone Block]
[Bone Block] [Bone Block] [Bone Block]
[Bone Block] [Bone Block] [Bone Block]
```
